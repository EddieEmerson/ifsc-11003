How do the rovers get power?

Newer rovers like Curiosity and Perseverance use nuclear batteries (MMRTGs) to stay powered during the cold Martian nights.


How long does it take to talk to a rover?

Depending on the planets' positions, it takes between 5 and 20 minutes for a radio signal to reach Earth.
