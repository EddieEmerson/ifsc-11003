Mission Overview

NASA's Mars Exploration Program is a science-driven strategy for exploring the fourth planet from the sun.

Curiosity

Launched in 2011 to determine if Mars was ever able to support microbial life. It found evidence of liquid water in the past.

Perseverance

The latest rover, searching for signs of ancient life and collecting rock samples for future return to Earth.
